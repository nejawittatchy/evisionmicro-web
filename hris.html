eVision HRIS System

Our Human Resource Information System (HRIS) streamlines HR processes, empowering businesses to manage employee data, payroll, recruitment, and performance in one integrated platform. With features like automated attendance tracking, seamless payroll management, and real-time reporting, our HRIS reduces administrative workload and enhances workforce efficiency.

Comprehensive HR Modules

Our HRIS helps HR teams make informed decisions, improve compliance, and boost employee satisfaction using these powerful integrated modules.

Employee Information Management

Securely store all employee records and assigned asset details in a centralized database.

  • Organization structure & Charts
  • Employee Profile Management
    • Personal info, Photograph, ID docs
    • Work, Contract, & Contact details
    • Qualification, Expertise, & History
  • Assets tracking (phones, laptops, etc.)
  • Quick Search & Criteria filtering
  • Expiring contract notifications

Employee Lifecycle Management

Track journeys from onboarding to retirement with full history and automated workflows.

  • Manage Promotions, Demotions, Increments
  • Transfers & Terminations
  • Resignations & Retirements
  • User-defined events support
  • Generate Letters & Attach Docs
  • Full Audit trail & Approval processes

Time Attendance

Interface with biometric hardware for precise and automated time tracking.

  • RFID/Fingerprint/Face Recognition support
  • Shift & Roster management
  • Regular vs Flexi-hours
  • OT limits and Overtime Authorization
  • Leave Encashment & Lieu Leaves
  • Late attendance penalties & Allowances

Payroll

Handle complex processing for multiple staff categories with automatic statutory compliance.

  • Staff, Laborer, Executive, & Director payroll
  • Attendance-based payment rules
  • Loan recording & Installments
  • Advance payments & Deductions
  • Electronic Bank File generation
  • Statutory Reports (EPF, ETF, PAYE, Tax)

Performance Management

Empower employees with transparent goal setting and continuous feedback.

  • KRA/KPI & Balanced Scorecard methods
  • 360 evaluation tools
  • Auto-reminders for reviews
  • Transparent PMS workflows
  • Skill gap identification & training prompts
  • Aggregate performance macro views

Training and Development

Nurture effective teams by integrating performance needs with training calendars.

  • Training Needs Assessment integration
  • Centralized Training Database & Calendar
  • Self-service training requests
  • Budgeting & Impact analysis
  • Feedback loops for Trainees & Trainers
  • Employer brand & loyalty building

Employee Self Service

A centralized portal for staff to manage their work life independently.

  • Profile & Contact updates
  • Company Calendar & FAQs
  • Leave & Attendance requests
  • Stationery & Document requests
  • Promotion/Transfer applications
  • Corporate news & notifications

BI & Analytical Reporting

Gain deep insights through a powerful drag-and-drop reporting interface.

  • Comprehensive Standard Reports
  • User-designed Custom Reports
  • Drag-and-Drop simple UI
  • Variation analysis over time
  • Competency & Performance matrices
  • Multi-dimensional comparisons
