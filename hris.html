Human Resource Information System (HRIS)

The eVision HRIS system delivers complete visibility and authority over your entire human resource management workflow. Developed through extensive research and real-world application, this platform guarantees seamless HR operations from recruitment to retirement.

Key Modules & Capabilities

Employee Information Management

Securely store all employee records and assigned asset details.

Employee Lifecycle Management

Keep track of staff promotions, transfers, and organizational exits.

Time Attendance

Accurately record employee shifts, manage leaves, and track time-related penalties.

Payroll

Seamlessly process employee salaries, handle deductions, and generate detailed payroll reports.

Performance Management

Conduct employee appraisals, track overarching goals, and assign Key Performance Indicators (KPIs).

Training and Development

Systematically plan and oversee employee training programs.

Employee Self Service

Empower staff to self-manage their profiles, request leave, and submit internal requests.

Business Intelligence & Reporting

Utilize custom HR dashboards and detailed analytical reports for workforce insights.
