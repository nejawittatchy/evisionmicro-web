Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)

The eVision ERP system is an intelligent solution designed to streamline operations, boost efficiency, and drive business growth. Built to accommodate businesses of all sizes, it integrates critical functions like finance, sales, and inventory to provide real-time insights and total control over your operations. By simplifying complex processes and reducing costs, this cutting-edge software empowers you to make smarter decisions and experience seamless automation.

Key Modules & Capabilities

Supplier Relationship Management

Efficiently manage your suppliers, related documents, payments, and reports.

Customer Relationship Management

Track customer information, agreements, and specific credit limits.

Inventory Control

Monitor your stock levels, quality assurance (QA), and overall item movement.

Purchasing

Maintain control over procurement processes, Goods Receipt Notes (GRN), and vendor payments.

Manufacturing

Effectively plan, track, and generate reports on your production cycles.

Sales

Handle everything from incoming orders and pricing to invoicing and returns.

Accounting

Manage financial ledgers, process payments, and generate comprehensive financial reports.

Business Intelligence

Analyze business performance, sales data, and emerging market trends.

Security Access Control

Strictly control system access and conduct audits of user activity.

Online Secure Backup & Reporting

Benefit from automated backups, online report generation, and system notifications.
