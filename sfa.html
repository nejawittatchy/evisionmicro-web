Sales Force Automation (SFA)

The eVision SFA system grants you complete visibility across your entire sales and distribution network. Battle-tested in the field and designed following rigorous field surveys, it significantly minimizes the delays, costs, and errors typically associated with manual processes. The software features a modularized architecture, allowing businesses to start with core functionalities and seamlessly add more modules as they grow.

Key Modules & Capabilities

CRM Module

Easily manage your customer base, available stock, pricing strategies, and ongoing sales.

Distributor Module

Handle operational tasks such as orders, invoicing, collections, and reporting.

Sales Module

Plan efficient sales routes, manage your sales operations, and handle localized reporting.

Merchandising Module

Keep track of store shelves, marketing campaigns, and available stock.
